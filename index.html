WE ARE DEFIANTS

Dive into the neon-drenched chaos of Neo-Tokyo in Project Z, a cyberpunk world on the precipice of collapse. Atura's oppressive regime clamps down on the city, squeezing the life out of its citizens. The once-vibrant resistance lies fractured, each clinging to a sliver of hope for a better tomorrow.

Hack into the heart of the machine with The Phantoms, waging a silent war with lines of code. Become a relentless Iron Raven, a master of explosives disrupting Atura's ironclad infrastructure. Or join Elysium, disillusioned enforcers with a taste for rebellion, sowing chaos from within the very system they once upheld.

Choose your side. Can you unite the fractured resistance or will you become another pawn in Atura's twisted game? Project Z awaits.
