We Are Defiant

Dive into the neon-drenched chaos of Neo-Tokyo in Project Z, a cyberpunk world teetering on the brink. Atura's iron grip chokes the city, and the resistance lies shattered. Hackers (The Phantoms) wage digital war, demolition experts (The Iron Ravens) disrupt the regime's infrastructure, and disillusioned enforcers (Elysium) sow discord from within. Choose your faction and fight. But can you overcome the fractures and unite them all, or will you become another pawn in Atura's game of control?
